Littlefoot

Littlefoot is a young male Apatosaurus, or “Longneck” as they are known in the series. His brown colouring and frame are very accurate to real-life Apatosaurus, but his head, neck and tail are inaccurate. Scientists model the Apatosaurus as having a cone-shaped head, and its neck and tail each being just as long as, if not longer, than their main bodies. The neck and tail length could be attributed to the animation style, but the head is still inaccurate. Overall, Littlefoot is a mostly accurate depiction of an Apatosaurus. I rate him a 4/5.




Cera

Cera is a young female Triceratops, or “Threehorn” as they are known in the series. Her colouring changes quite a lot over the series, ranging from light tan to bright orange, and none of the spectrum of colours she is portrayed as are accurate. Scientists model the Triceratops as being a much darker, gray-brown colour. Other than her colouring, her missing horns and low amount of spikes on her frill are much stranger discrepancies. Her frame is the only accurate attribute of her design. Overall, Cera is a very inaccurate depiction of a Triceratops. I rate her a 2/5.
